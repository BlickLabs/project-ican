Who are we?

We are a non-profit group of doctors, lawyers, pharmacists, scientists, lobbyists and concerned citizens that are devoted and dedicated to bringing forward the full benefits of the Medical Cannabis industry into the health system helping to reshape the healthcare landscape with proper regulations, scientific studies and social responsibility.

Leading Scientific

Leading Scientific,
Regulatory and Technical Expert.

ICAN is the leading expert regarding Medical Cannabis in the region. We strive to make the process of finding information that is publicly accessible in a fast, simple, and transparent manner.
We are also focused on helping authorities, medical and pharmaceutical thought leaders, as well as business-owners come together for better understanding of the beneficial aspects and social impact of the Medical Cannabis.
